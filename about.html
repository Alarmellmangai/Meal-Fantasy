OUR STORY


Meal Fantasy is sprouting its sapling in the beautiful, vibrant city of chennai. We serve the most authentic, traditional Indian food, providing high standards in service and quality.We have been serving for past 10 years. For us meal time is more than food-it's about connection and sharing love. We believe in supporting other local businesses as well. Our mission is to provide you with the deepest pleasure of quality dining and exceptional service. Our sincere effort is to make your dining experience memorable by bringing to you centuries old Cuisine, and recipes from our mother's kitchen. We provide an excellent choice of various exotic vegetables, lentils, breads, aromatic rice, meat and much more. Our extensive menu includes a fine collection of wine and spirits in addition to well-known traditional Indian dishes.
